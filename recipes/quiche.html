Quiche

This quiche recipe is a creamy ham and broccoli delight!

This quiche works as a meal for any time of day. Though honestly.. The best time to eat it is at 4am, don't you think?

This delicious dish is simple, quick, and ready in a blink of an eye.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Biscuit Baking Mix
  • Butter
  • Broccoli
  • Cheese
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Beat eggs, milk, cheese, baking mix, and butter into a large bowl
  3. Pour mixture into quiche dish
  4. Bake in oven for about 50 minutes
