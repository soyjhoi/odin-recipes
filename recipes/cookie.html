Chocolate Chip Cookie

These are delicious and gooey chocolate chip cookies!

Chocolate chip cookies are hands down the best kind of cookies. This is a hill that I am willing to collapse on.

This recipe is super beginner friendly! Simple and a delight to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and Vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to mix
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips and nuts
  4. Drop the dough onto prepped baking sheet
  5. Bake until edges browned.
