Pancake

These are the perfect pancakes to start off your morning! Fluffy, soft and tasty.

Pancakes are honestly so flipping based. Like honestly, way better than waffles imo, IDEK about you but these are a bangaaaaa.

This recipe is super simple. All you gotta do is mix the ingredients up, pop it over the pan, and voila! Congrats! you've got yourself some delicious pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients
  2. Make a well and add those wet ingredients in
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle
  4. Look out for bubbles. Cook for a couple minutes then flip over
  5. Continue cooking till browned on both sides/li>
